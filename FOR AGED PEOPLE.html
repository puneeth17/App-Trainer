Step by Step plan

More Pain More Gain.


EX 01

Exercise,

01.

* Sleep on the Mat..
* Keep your neck raised over the ground..
* Touch the heel with respective hand..

EX 02

Exercise,

02.

* Place your elbows on the Ball..
* Do not bend your legs..
* Push the ball forward and backward..

EX 03

Exercise,

03.

* Lay down on the floor..
* Lift just your legs vertically up and down..
* Relax Slowly..

EX 04

Exercise,

04.

* Lay down on the floor..
* Keep exchanging the ball from Legs to Hands..
* Relax Slowly..

EX 05

Exercise,

05.

* Sit on a bench..
* Keep your one arm on the ground as shown in the fig..
* Move your legs from left to right 180 degrees..

EX 06

Exercise,

06.

* Rest your body on the floor with your forearms and toes touching..
* Lift one leg each at a time and do it repeatedly..
* Relax Slowly..

EX 07

Exercise,

07.

* Place your arms on the floor and lift your body with toes touching..
* Lift one leg such that the knee is almost touching your chest..
* Repeat the same with the second leg..

EX 08

Exercise,

08.

* Lay down on the floor with your back resting..
* Move your legs like we do in the cycling..
* Relax.

EX 09

Exercise,

09.

* Lay down on the floor with your back resting..
* Fold one leg and then straight it up..
* Repeat the process again..





EX 10

Exercise,

10.

* Lay down on the floor..
* Place your hands at the back of the neck..
* Move your hands and legs in such a way that left elbow touches right leg knee..

EX 11

Exercise,

11.

* Lay down on the floor..
* Just fold your legs and move your body vertically upwards as shown above..
* Repeat this for 15 mins..

EX 12

Exercise,

12.

* Take a barrel..
* Roll it by holding in the hands on the floor..
* Do not move your feet..

EX 13

Exercise,

13.

* Take a rod..
* Lay down on the floor and keep it moving from your feet to hip as shown in the fig..
* Relax for sometime and repeat.

EX 14

Exercise,

14.

* Lay down on the floor..
* Rest your back and move your legs vertically up in a criss cross way..
* Relax.

EX 15

Exercise,

15.

* Lay down on the floor..
* Lift both your legs vertically upwards and down. Repeat this process..
