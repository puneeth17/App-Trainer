List of Workout for : ABS



Step by Step plan for Abs

More Pain More Gain.


EX 01

Abs,

01.

* Kneel down on glideboard, grasp the toe with hands and push your body up the rail while keeping your thighs.
* Pull yourself back towards the toe bar while keeping your arms and upper body fully extended.
* Be careful not to back yourself too far out up to a point where you would lose balance.

EX 02

Abs,

02.

* Lie down on your back with your head down, feet attched to the wing attachment, knees slightly bent.
* Roll your shoulder blades up from glideboard, At same time pull yourself up closer.
* To avoid pulling your neck with your hands, look straight up instead of looking at your knees.

EX 03

Abs,

03.

* Kneel down on the glideboard, grasp the base of the machine with your hands and push your body up.
* Pull yourself back towards your hands by bending your hips and knees and push yourself back up.
* Keep your arms extended and perpendicular to the floor throughout.

EX 04

Abs,

04.

* Sit down on the glideboard looking up, knees bent and hold the handles with your hands.
* Roll your upper body up until your lower back no longer touches the glideboard and lower yourself back down after a short pause.
* To avoid pulling your neck with your hands, look straight up instead of looking at your knees.

EX 05

Abs,

05.

* Sit sideways on the glideboard, keep your legs extended up from the floor and hold the handles with both hands.
* Pull the handles towards the other side of your trunk and allow yourself back to the starting position.
* Keep your back straight throughout.

EX 06

Abs,

06.

* Lie down on your back with your knees bent and your feet on the glideboard and hold the handles with your hands.
* Roll your shoulder blades up from the glideboard by pushing on the handles while keeping your arms extended.
* To avoid pulling your neck with your hands, look straight up instead of looking at your knees.



List of Workout for : BICEPS



Step by Step plan for Biceps

More Pain More Gain.


EX 01

Biceps

01

* Sit on the glideboard, legs extended and tilt your upper body forward to hold handles above your ankles.
* Slide the glideboard up by pulling the handles towards your shoudlers while raising your upper body.
* Breathe out while pulling the handles and breathe in while returning to starting position.

EX 02

Biceps

02

* Sit on the glideboard and hold the handles with your hands above your knees, arms extended.
* Slide the glideboard up by pulling handles towards your shoulders while curling your legs.
* Keep your upper arms in the same position throughout.

EX 03

Biceps

03

* Lie on your back on the glideboard, knees bent, and hold the handles with your arms extended.
* Slide the glideboard up by pulling handles towards shoulders and allow yourself back down.
* Keep your upper arms pressed against the slideboard throughout.

EX 04

Biceps,

04.

* Sit on the glideboard, knees slightly bent and hold the handles down below your buttocks.
* Slide the glideboard up by pulling the handle towards your shoulder and allow yourself back down.
* Keep your upper arm in the same position throughout.

EX 05

Biceps

05.

* Lie on your back on the glideboard, knees bent, palms facing up and raise your upper body from up the mat.
* Slide the glideboard up by pulling the handles towards your shoulders and allow yourself back down.
* Keep your upper body off the glideboard and your upper arms in the same position throughout.

EX 06

Biceps

06.

* Sit on the glideboard and hold the handles down above your ankles, arms and legs extended.
* Slide the glideboard up by pulling the handles and allow yourself back down after a short pause.
* Keep your upper arms in the same position throughout.

04.

* Keep your arms fully extended throughout (or keep the small bend in your elbows constant).

EX 05

Shoulder,

05.

* Slide the glideboard up by pulling the handles up towards your shoulders while curling your legs.

EX 06

Shoulder,

06.

* Sit on the top end of the glideboard and hold the handles above your knees, arms extended.
* Slide the glideboard up by pulling the handles up towards your shoulders and allow yourself back down.
* Keep your back straight throughout.

List of Workout for : LEGS



Step by Step plan for LEGS

More Pain More Gain.


EX 01

Legs,

01.

* Lie on glideboard with your legs extended and attach your ankles to wing attachment.
* Roll your shoulder blades up from the gildeboard while keeping your lower back.
* Breathe out while curling your legs and breathe in while returning to starting position.

EX 02

Legs,

02.

* Lie on your back on the glideboard, hips and knees at 90 degree angles.
* Raise your buttocks from up the glideboard by straightening your hips.
* Keep a 90 degree angle in your knees throughout.

EX 03

Legs,

03.

* Lie on your back on the glideboard with your feet on the squat stand.
* Slide the glideboard up by straightening your legs and allow yourself back down.
* Breathe out while straightening your legs and breathe in while returning to starting position.

EX 04

Legs,

04.

* Kneel on one knee on the glideboard with the other foot resting against the squat stand.
* Slide the glideboard up by straightening your leg and allow back down after a short pause.
* Breathe out while straightening your leg and breathe in while returning to starting position.

EX 05

Legs,

05.

* Lie sideways on the glideboard with one foot on the squat stand, its knee at a 90 degree angle.
* Slide the glideboard up by straightening your leg and allow yourself back down after a short pause.
* Breathe out while straightening your leg and breathe in while returning to starting position.

EX 06

Legs,

06.

* Lie on your back on the glideboard with one foot on the squat stand with its knee at a 90 degree angles.
* Slide the glideboard up by straightening your leg and allow yourself back down after a short pause.
* Breathe out while straightening your leg and breathe in while returning to starting position.
