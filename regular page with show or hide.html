Regular Workout Schedule

WEEK 1 PLAN



Day 01 to Day 07

Step by Step plan for week 1

More Pain More Gain.


DAY 01

Crunch,

01.

* Lie down on your back with your feet pressed against the squat stand.
* Roll your shoulder blades up from the gildeboard while keeping your lower back.
* To avoid pulling your neck with your hands, look straight up instead of looking at your knees.

DAY 01

Crunch,

02.

* Lie down on your back with your head down, feet attched to the wing attachment.
* Roll your shoulder blades up from glideboard, At same time pull yourself up closer.
* To avoid pulling your neck with your hands, look straight up instead of looking at your knees.

DAY 01

Crunch,

03.

* Lie down on your back with your head down, knees slightly bent.
* Roll your shoulder up from glideboard and lower yourself back down after short pause.
* To avoid pulling your neck with your hands, look straight up instead of looking at your knees.

DAY 03

Back,

01.

* Lie on your back on the glideboard,feet on squat stand with your knees bent.
* Slide the glideboard up by pulling on handles towards abdomen while extending legs.
* Keep the small bend in your elbows constant throughout.

DAY 03

Back,

02.

* Sit on the glideboard with your legs fully extended and crouch down slowly.
* Slide the glideboard up while raising your upper body by pulling handles towards the sides.
* Breathe out while raising and breathe in while lowering yourself back.

DAY 03

Back,

03.

* Sit on the top edge of the glideboard and hold the handles with your hands.
* Slide the glideboard up by pulling on one handle towards its side of your body.
* Keep your arms fully extended (or keep the small bend in your elbows constant).

DAY 05

Chest,

01.

* Lie face down with your hands on the glideboard, back and legs fully extended and elbows bent.
* Push yourself up by extending your arms and allow yourself back down after short pause.
* Keep your back and legs fully extended throughout.

DAY 05

Chest,

02.

* Sit on the glideboard with your knees bent and hold handles with hands on each side of chest.
* Slide up by pulling the handles close to each other, And back down after a short pause.
* Keep the small bend in your elbows constant throughout.

DAY 05

Chest,

03.

* Sit sideways with your legs extended out ,arm extended out with elbow only slightly bent.
* Slide the glideboard up by pulling the handle towards the front of your chest.
* Keep the small bend in your elbows constant throughout.

DAY 07

Legs,

01.

* Lie on glideboard with your legs extended and attach your ankles to wing attachment.
* Roll your shoulder blades up from the gildeboard while keeping your lower back.
* Breathe out while curling your legs and breathe in while returning to starting position.

DAY 07

Legs,

02.

* Lie on your back on the glideboard, hips and knees at 90 degree angles.
* Raise your buttocks from up the glideboard by straightening your hips.
* Keep a 90 degree angle in your knees throughout.

DAY 07

Legs,

03.

* Lie on your back on the glideboard with your feet on the squat stand.
* Slide the glideboard up by straightening your legs and allow yourself back down.
* Breathe out while straightening your legs and breathe in while returning to starting position.



WEEK 2 PLAN



Day 08 to Day 15

Step by Step plan for week 2

More Pain More Gain.


DAY 01

Biceps

01

* Sit on the glideboard, legs extended and tilt your upper body forward to hold handles above your ankles.
* Slide the glideboard up by pulling the handles towards your shoudlers while raising your upper body.
* Breathe out while pulling the handles and breathe in while returning to starting position.

DAY 01

Biceps

02

* Sit on the glideboard and hold the handles with your hands above your knees, arms extended.
* Slide the glideboard up by pulling handles towards your shoulders while curling your legs.
* Keep your upper arms in the same position throughout.

DAY 01

Biceps

03

* Lie on your back on the glideboard, knees bent, and hold the handles with your arms extended.
* Slide the glideboard up by pulling handles towards shoulders and allow yourself back down.
* Keep your upper arms pressed against the slideboard throughout.

DAY 03

Shoulder,

01.

* Sit on the glideboard, knees slightly bent and hold the handles down below your buttocks.
* Slide the glideboard up by pulling the handles forward until your arms are parallel to the floor.
* Keep your arms fully extended throughout (or keep the small bend in your elbows constant).

DAY 03

Shoulder

02.

* Lie face down on the glideboard, knees bent and grasp the press-up bars with your hands.
* Slide the glideboard up by extending your arms and allow yourself back down after a short pause.
* Breathe out while pushing and breathe in while returning to starting position.

DAY 03

Shoulder

03.

* Sit on the glideboard and hold the handles down above your ankles, arms and legs extended.
* Slide the glideboard up by pulling the handles up towards your shoulders while raising your back.
* Breathe out while raising yourself and breathe in while lowering yourself back.

DAY 05

Triceps

01.

* Kneel on the glideboard and hold the handles in front of your chest, elbows bent.
* Slide the glideboard up by pulling the handles back, straightening your arms and allow yourself back down.
* Keep your upper arms and upper body in the same position throughout.

DAY 05

Triceps,

02.

* Sit sideways on the glideboard and hold one handle with the hand furthest to it in front of your chest.
* Slide the glideboard up by pulling the handle back, straightening your arm and allow yourself back down.
* Keep your upper arm and upper body in the same position throughout.

DAY 05

Triceps,

03.

* Lie prone on the glideboard and hold the handles with your hands below your shoulders.
* Slide the glideboard up by pulling the handles back towards the sides of your buttocks, straightening your arms.
* Keep your upper arms in the same position throughout.

DAY 07

Sit-Up,

01.

* Lie down on your back with your feet pressed against the squat stand and extend your arms up.
* Roll your upper body up until your lower back no longer touches the glideboard and until your arms are parallel to your legs.
* Try to keep your back, legs and arms straight throughout.

DAY 07

Low Leg Pull-In

02.

* Kneel down on the glideboard, grasp the base of the machine with your hands and push your body up the rail.
* Pull yourself back towards your hands by bending your hips and knees and push yourself back up after a short pause.
* Keep your arms extended and perpendicular to the floor throughout.

DAY 07

Sit-Up with Cable

03.

* Sit down on the glideboard looking up, knees bent and hold the handles with your hands.
* Roll your upper body up until your lower back no longer touches the glideboard and lower yourself back down after a short pause.
* To avoid pulling your neck with your hands, look straight up instead of looking at your knees.
